Now

This about page is an idea from Derek Sivers, it tells you what I am working on right now:

Enjoying and working on my new old house⌗

Last spring we moved to a cozy little house far outside of the city. As the weather cools I am working on more and more inside the house. The small neighborhood vibe and Christmas lights, my first fireplace all make the winter more pleasant.

Celebrating the holidays with friends and family⌗

After last years pause on parties, I was grateful to be able to see my family for the holidays again this year. I recently went to the Art Institute with friends I haven’t seen for a while. This was probably the longest stretch of not visiting the Art Institute as well.

Reflecting on the year and planning next⌗

This is the time of year, I start to review what I have done this year and what I had hoped to have done. This year already looks like a win in many catagories.

Writing⌗

Setting up this new site on Hugo. I chose Hugo for it’s speed and the fact that it’s open source. After a break, I am back to writing about things that interest me and take me down rabit holes. This is where I will post them.

Planning garden for next spring⌗

There is a lot of work to be done before anything can be planted. The goal is to balance productive fertility and an aesthetically pleasing retreat.

Rehabbing knee pain from running⌗

I started running a couple years ago and finally got the hang of it this summer. Then I went to far and got an overuse injury. Trying to rehab it myself with information from others, books and youtube.
